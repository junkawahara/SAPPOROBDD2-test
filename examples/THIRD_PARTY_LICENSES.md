## bdd-benchmark

**Repository**: https://github.com/ssoelvsten/bdd-benchmark

The following example files are based on bdd-benchmark:
- `nqueens.cpp` - N-Queens problem solver
- `tictactoe.cpp` - 4x4x4 Tic-Tac-Toe draw counter
- `hamiltonian.cpp` - Hamiltonian cycle finder
- `gameoflife.cpp` - Conway's Game of Life
- `cnf.cpp` - DIMACS CNF SAT solver
- `apply.cpp` - BDD apply benchmark
- `relprod.cpp` - Relational product benchmark
